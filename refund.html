Refund Policy

Last updated: 30 April 2025 · Operated by Protocol Zero Limited

We stand behind our service. If you're not satisfied with your analysis, we'll make it right — including a full refund if we can't resolve your concerns.

1. Our Satisfaction Guarantee

We want you to feel confident using Verihome NZ. If you are not satisfied with the quality of your analysis report, please contact us within 7 days of purchase and we will work to resolve your concern, including offering a full refund where appropriate.

2. Eligible Refund Situations

You are entitled to a refund in the following circumstances:

  • The analysis report was not delivered within the stated turnaround time (48 hours for Essential, 24 hours for Complete, 12 hours for Premium)
  • The analysis is clearly incorrect or incoherent (e.g., analysis of the wrong document type)
  • A technical error prevented delivery of your report
  • You are genuinely dissatisfied with the quality of the analysis and contact us within 7 days
  • You are entitled to a refund under the Consumer Guarantees Act 1993 (NZ)

3. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds are generally not provided in the following situations:

  • You change your mind after receiving a complete, accurate report
  • You uploaded an incorrect or unreadable document and the analysis was completed based on what was provided
  • More than 7 days have passed since purchase (unless Consumer Guarantees Act applies)
  • The property purchase proceeded and you disagree with our assessment in hindsight

4. Consumer Guarantees Act 1993

If you are a consumer under the Consumer Guarantees Act 1993 (NZ), you have statutory rights that cannot be limited by this policy. If our service fails to meet the consumer guarantee of reasonable care and skill, you are entitled to have the problem remedied or, if the failure is substantial, to a refund. These rights are in addition to, and not limited by, this policy.

5. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund:

  • Email us at support@verihome.co.nz
  • Include your order reference number (from your Stripe receipt email)
  • Briefly describe the reason for your refund request

We aim to respond to all refund requests within 2 business days. Approved refunds are processed via the original payment method and typically appear within 5-10 business days depending on your bank.

6. Partial Refunds

In some cases, we may offer a partial refund or credit where a full refund is not warranted but some service deficiency occurred. We will communicate this clearly when applicable.

7. Launch Offer Pricing

Refunds are processed based on the amount actually paid (launch offer price), not the original listed price. Promotional discounts and promo code amounts are not refunded separately.

8. Contact Us

For refund requests or questions about this policy:
Email: support@verihome.co.nz
We aim to respond within 2 business days (Monday–Friday, NZ time).
