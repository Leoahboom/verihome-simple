Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 April 2025 · Operated by Protocol Zero Limited

Your privacy matters. We collect only what we need to provide the service, we do not sell your data, and your property documents are processed securely and deleted after analysis.

1. Who We Are

Verihome NZ is a service operated by Protocol Zero Limited, a New Zealand company. We provide AI-powered property document analysis for New Zealand homebuyers. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information in accordance with the Privacy Act 2020 (NZ).

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use our service:

  • Documents you upload: Sale & Purchase Agreements, LIM Reports, Building Inspection Reports, and other property documents. These may contain personal information such as names, addresses, and property details.
  • Contact information: Email address provided at Stripe checkout for payment confirmation and report delivery.
  • Payment information: Processed entirely by Stripe. We do not store your credit card details.
  • Usage data: Basic analytics including pages visited, browser type, and general location (country/region) for service improvement.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Analyse your property documents and generate your report
  • Process your payment via Stripe
  • Send you your analysis report by email
  • Improve our AI analysis quality (using anonymised, aggregated data only)
  • Comply with our legal obligations

We do not use your data for advertising, sell your data to third parties, or share it with real estate agents or other parties without your explicit consent.

4. Document Storage & Deletion

Your uploaded documents are processed in a secure environment (Vercel serverless functions) and stored temporarily in memory only during processing. Documents are not retained on our servers after analysis is complete. Analysis results are retained for up to 30 days to allow report re-delivery if needed, then permanently deleted.

5. AI Processing

Your document text is sent to OpenAI's API for analysis. OpenAI processes this data according to their API data usage policies. As of the date of this policy, OpenAI does not use API-submitted data to train their models. We send only the relevant extracted text — not your original files — to OpenAI.

6. Cookies & Tracking

We use minimal cookies necessary for the website to function. We do not use third-party advertising cookies. Basic analytics may use session cookies to understand aggregate usage patterns.

7. Your Rights Under the Privacy Act 2020

Under the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020, you have the right to:

  • Access personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of any inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Complain to the Office of the Privacy Commissioner if you believe your privacy rights have been breached

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@verihome.co.nz.

8. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including TLS encryption for all data in transit, secure Vercel infrastructure for serverless processing, and Stripe's PCI-DSS compliant payment handling. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, but we take all reasonable steps to protect your information.

9. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Stripe — Payment processing (Stripe Privacy Policy)
  • OpenAI — AI document analysis (OpenAI API Policy)
  • Vercel — Website hosting and serverless functions (Vercel Privacy Policy)

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting a notice on our website. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at:
